Totals measured in minutes

We found a dataset in Kaggle that included data from the Department of Transportation, with Domestic flight data for 2015. The dataset was close to 1.6GB and consisted of 31 columns, and 5.8 million rows.

The Cleaning



Previous Next


The cleanup consisted of the following:

  • Consolidate some airport codes showing 5 characters to 3.
  • Group outgoing flights per airport to understand the importance of each.
  • Group delays per airport to understand which airport tends to have more delays.
  • Only include delays greater than 10 minutes, to avoid having many outliers.
  • Made summary tables (smaller CSV) to avoid working with the massive file.
  • Loaded individual tables into SQLite.
